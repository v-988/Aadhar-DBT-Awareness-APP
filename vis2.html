Our Mission & Vision

Know about Aadhaar-Linked Account & DBT-Enabled Aadhaar-Seeded Account

Aadhaar-Linked Account Secure

Linked to Aadhaar for robust identity verification and seamless access to government services. Essential for KYC compliance and Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) eligibility with enhanced security features.

  • Enhanced fraud prevention and security
  • Unified identity across all government services
  • Paperless documentation and storage
  • Real-time authentication capabilities

DBT-Enabled Aadhaar-Seeded Account Primary

Designated by your bank as the primary account for Direct Benefit Transfers (DBT). This account receives government benefit payments, scholarships, and subsidies directly through the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) routing system.

Click to explore DBT benefits
Critical Importance

This account is essential for receiving direct scholarship transfers, government subsidies, and social welfare payments. NPCI automatically routes all government benefit payments to your DBT-enabled account, ensuring you don't miss any financial assistance.

Key Advantages

  • Guaranteed receipt of all government benefit transfers
  • Eliminates payment delays and processing bottlenecks
  • Prevents fund diversion and leakage in the system
  • Automatic routing of scholarship and subsidy payments
  • Real-time payment notifications and confirmations
  • Transparent transaction tracking and audit trail
  • Reduces dependency on intermediaries and agents
  • Direct credit without manual intervention

Core Features

Step-by-Step: How to enable DBT and check seeding

  1. Visit bank branch with Aadhaar & passbook or use UMANG / bank netbanking.
  2. Ask staff to seed Aadhaar (link) to your account (KYC).
  3. Ask bank to mark the account as DBT-enabled / primary for benefits.
  4. Take acknowledgement slip and verify on scholarship portal before deadlines.

Tip: If multiple accounts are linked, only the DBT-enabled one receives the payment.
