---

## 🎯 Project Overview

FinAware is a web-based awareness platform developed to help students understand the critical difference between:

- **Aadhaar-Linked Bank Accounts**
- **DBT-Enabled Aadhaar-Seeded Bank Accounts**

The platform uses **interactive quizzes, gamification, instant feedback, and engaging UI/UX** to simplify complex financial literacy concepts related to scholarships and government benefit transfers.

## 🧠 Key Concepts Covered

### 🔹 Aadhaar-Linked Bank Account
A bank account where Aadhaar is simply linked with the bank account.

### 🔹 DBT-Enabled Aadhaar-Seeded Account
A bank account enabled for receiving government subsidies, scholarships, and welfare benefits directly through the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) scheme.
